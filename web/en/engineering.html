Home / Engineering

Using 3D design and simulation tools we offer a wide range of products for a very specific sector such as hot rolling, with emphasis on all types of equipment.

Our main occupation is the development of custom machinery projects at different scales, from the design of a rolling mill train to the design of a simple machine component.

Due to the great experience accumulated in the realization of multiple custom projects, we currently offer our services successfully to sectors other than steel, such as the automotive sector.
